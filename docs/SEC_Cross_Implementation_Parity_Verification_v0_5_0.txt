1. PURPOSE

SEC uses independent implementation agreement as one verification layer.

Parity does not establish universal truth. It checks whether separately implemented resolution paths agree over frozen bounded corpora.

6. PARITY COVERAGE

Exact core parity covers the frozen 38-vector core and identity metadata.

Structural innovation parity covers:

RESOLVED_POINT stability
RESOLVED_REGION stability
INCOMPLETE stability
CONFLICT stability
structural differential states
symmetry admission and refusal
resolution certificates
innovation vector-set and corpus identities

Advanced centre parity covers:

claim relations
result relations
centre spectra
resolution frontiers
exact graph-centre outcomes
advanced vector-set and corpus identities

Structural portability parity covers:

shared generic claim-relation semantics
shared result-spectrum semantics
shared resolution-frontier semantics
shared resolution-certificate binding
two bounded domain adapters
portability vector-set and corpus identities

7. BOUNDARIES

Core parity does not claim that the browser independently recomputes the polygon dataset.

Innovation parity does not claim that every future stability-region representation is covered.

Advanced parity is bounded to the frozen claim-relation, spectrum, frontier, and graph-centre vectors.

Portability parity is bounded to the generic kernel profile, two declared adapters, and frozen portability corpus.

Parity agreement does not establish universal correctness or third-party verification.
